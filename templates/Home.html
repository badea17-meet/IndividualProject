Frequently Asked Questions
How does the free trial work?

Our 30 day trial is completely free and does not require credit card information to start. If at the end of your trial you would like to upgrade, great. If not, you can cancel your account altogether, or let us mark it as inactive for you to come back to later.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can switch between our paid plans, or cancel your account altogether, whenever you like. We will adjust any payments accordingly.

Can I cancel a booked session?

Yes, you need to send a message to the dietitian you booked with in order to cancel the session.

What payment types do you accept?

We accept payments from MasterCard, Visa, Visa Debit and American Express. We do not accept PayPal. Remember, you do not need to supply card details to start your free trial.
