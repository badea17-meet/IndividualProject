FatFit is a multi-sided marketplace that connects dietitians with clients who seek weight loss and healthy journey.

The moment a client signs in, he can appoint online session with a dietitian he chooses. The decision of picking a dietitian is made by the clients, who chooses the dietitians that suits him best, according to dietitians’ areas of expertise and their years of experience showed by the website.

As for the dietitians, they also can register themselves, and serve a wide range on clients from all over the world. A dietitian needs to open available future sessions, and in turn, a client picks this slot and books it.

All FatFit users, clients or dietitians, can enjoy the articles posted by FatFit, and also use the BMI calculator provided.
